The science behind specific frequency music have been proven to mood your mood and assist in whatever you want to accomplish. We have a range of frequency music including alpha, beta and gamma.

Recent studies show that the brain responds to certain music frequencies which can enhance multitudinous functions of the brain, these including decreasing levels of depression. Music therapy involves listening to music at certain frequencies which can stimulate the growth of neurotrophins in the brain. Certain music frequencies help with certain functions as shown below: alpha frequencies (8 to 13 Hz) – lowering stress, reducing anxiety, filtering out distracting sensory information, decreasing depression. beta frequencies ( 14Hz or greater) – increased focus, concentration and motivation, better long term memory, improved confidence gamma frequencies ( 25 - 140Hz ) - achieve peak concentration, attention to detail, divergent thinking .
